What is Customizable Access Control?

Customizable access control empowers retailers to assign permissions dynamically. Depending on job roles—such as store managers, sales associates, inventory clerks, or finance teams—iAmX lets you specify exactly what data and operations are accessible. By controlling access:

  • Security risks from unauthorized data exposure are minimized.
  • Employees see only information relevant to their tasks, improving clarity and focus.
  • Operational efficiency is boosted through role-specific workflows.
